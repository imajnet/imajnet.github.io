ImajNet is now FreeDoc! Founded in 1999 by Peter Frix, CDIA, CEIRM, ImajNet grew and expanded quickly, acquiring several local records management companies including Western Microfilm and FreeDoc.

Now operating under the name FreeDoc, the company uses twenty-two years of records management experience to offer a complete package of records management services including Enterprise Content Management software sales, setup, and maintenance, records management consulting services for public and private organizations through FreeDoc Consulting, and records capture and storage services through FreeDoc Capture & Conversion.
