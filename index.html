Enterprise Content Management Improves Financial Operations

Starting workflow within an approved Enterprise Content Management solution improves productivity an average of 30%. Workflow starts with submission of a web based e-form, an entry in the accounting system, or a scanned document.

Artificially intelligent scanning software is able to “lift” data from a documents and use it to populate a business application. Leveraging the native power of digital records, is a key step to making improvement. Scanning into Microsoft Office SharePoint Server (MOSS) is an effective method to improvement business process management. For workflow and report management (COLD), as a wider ECM solution, electronic records, efficiently categorized by a defined process reduce expense immediately.

For compliance as a DoD 5015.2 certified ECM, there are but a few solutions in the world able to meet the challenging requirements, such as “portability over time”.
